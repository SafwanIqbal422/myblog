Globalization and Its Effect on Skilled Workforce Migration

Globalization has made it easier for skilled workers to find opportunities abroad, intensifying brain drain in developing countries like Pakistan. While this opens doors for international career growth, it also creates a challenge for the home country to retain talent.

Global Recruitment

Companies and governments worldwide actively recruit skilled professionals, making it crucial for Pakistan to create competitive job opportunities.

Retention Strategies

Developing policies that provide incentives for skilled workers to return or contribute remotely can help mitigate this issue.

Modern Solutions

Encouraging remote work opportunities, freelancing platforms, and government-backed tech hubs can allow professionals to work for global firms while staying in Pakistan.
